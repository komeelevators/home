Elevator Cabin



As a general feature, our SAHARA model elevator cabin is designed by membrane coating.

Cabin Interior Decoration

Interior coatings of our manufactured SAHARA elevator cabin consist of membrane coating pressed on 18 mm MDF. Membrane coatings are designed by laying horizontally. Interior coating colors of our elevator cabin can be changed according to the preferences of our customers.

Elevator Cabin Interior Accessories

In our SAHARA model elevator cabin, super mirror stainless steel was placed between the membrane coverings as an accessory. The dimensions of the super mirror stainless steel are made different from each other, ensuring the integrity of the interior of the cabin.

Elevator Cabin Mirror

A full height 6 mm bisote mirror is used in the rear wall of our elevator cabin.

Elevator Cabin Base

The ground part of our SAHARA model elevator cabin is made of special natural granite stone flooring of 20 mm thickness. Standard granite stone and PVC floor covering can also be applied to the elevator floor according to customer preference.

Elevator Cabin Ceiling

In our SAHARA model elevator cabin internal electrical installations and lighting are ready to use in the elevator ceiling. The ceiling of our elevator cabin is designed as a hinged system with internal opening. In this way, the lighting inside the elevator ceiling can be easily changed from inside the cabin without going over the cabin. The elevator ceiling consists of laser-cut mirror stainless steel with a pattern. The interior of the cabin is colored with spot lighting.

Elevator Cabin Handrail

The interior handle of our elevator cabin consists of two stainless handrails. Thanks to the handrail fixing application in our elevator cabins, the loosening that may occur over time can be easily removed from the elevator cabin by tightening the handrail bolt. At the same time, if it is desired to change the handrail, it can be easily changed from inside the elevator cabin.

Elevator Cabin Ventilation

There are ventilation holes at the bottom of the elevator cabin walls in the interior ventilation system of our elevator cabin. In addition, there is a 220 V 12x12 square fan ventilation system inside the cabin ceiling. A special fan system of 220 V 15/60 providing stronger air flow can be made upon request.

Other Features

The fact that our SAHARA Model elevator cabin walls are an external plug-in system provides ease of installation in the difficulties that occur during installation. Along with the elevator cabin, standard bolted guardrail, skirt sheet, tube magnetic sheet, flexible chock connection sheet and guardrail foot strike sheet are delivered.

Usage places

Our SAHARA model elevator cabin is more preferred in residences, villas, hotels and workplaces.
