Capsule Elevators



These lifts can be installed on the exterior face of the building or in the lobby. The installation of these lifts provides totally changed view to the building. The technology used in these lifts is high-tech.

The range of lifts is from 8 persons to 40 persons for a three-sided glass capsule. 5 sided glass capsule is available for a minimum raise size of 13 persons. Octagonal shaped lifts are also being designed. Bottom lit marble floors, bell shaped roofs and cabin exteriors can be provided.

Our range of capsule lift cabin manufacturer product is widely used in hospitals, shopping malls, multiplexes and other residential & commercial buildings. We use qualitative capsule lift cabin manufacturer material in order to fabricate our products. Integrated with glass viewing panel, our capsule lift cabin is aesthetically designed in a capsule-like shape, hence the name. Our capsule lift cabin is exquisite in design and gives a panoramic view of outer surroundings through its glass walls. These capsule lift cabin exporter products are available in different sizes and shapes and can also be customized as according to specific requirements. Safety, security and beauty are the utmost factors kept under consideration while manufacturing this capsule lift cabin exporter product.
