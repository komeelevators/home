Control Panel



With the efforts of our professionals, we are able to manufacture and supply a broad range of Lift Control Panels that is based on latest technology. These panels are manufactured from high quality components procured from reliable sources of the industry and cutting edge machinery which ensure high performance at its user end. The entire range of Lift Control Panels is recognized for its reliability, easy operation and longer service life. Further, this range is programmable at the site as per the requirements and applicable in micro processor controller, dual type controller, etc.

• Micro Processor Controller
• Down Collective
• Dual Type Controller
• Selective & Collective Type
• V3F Micro Processor Panel
• APBC Dual Control

Features:

• Attractive enclosure for larger application
• Micro controller based state of the art design
• Machine Room & Room-less type Enclosure
• Highly noise Immune circuit design and stable power supply backing to withstand all type of power supply conditions.
• Digital Filter technique for all Inputs to avoid malfunctioning due to nuisance input signals.
• Field serviceable socket type inter connectivity & Modular PC- Boards for hassle free replacement.
• Series type of Field Interlocking safety chain for achieving Highest Safety.
• Two Tire safety interlocking.
• Digital Position/Direction Indicator in Control Panel to monitor the car movement.
• Separate LED Indication for Inputs & Outputs for quick debugging.
• Ramp Type CAM activation & Deactivation for noiseless operation.
• Intelligent Electronics stop Functionality.
• By-Pass / Non Stop facility to override registered call priority.
• Auto Mode / Attendant Mode operation.
• Auto Fan / Light operation for Energy Saving.
• Audio Buzzer For Open door condition in manually operated door Lifts.
• Fire man operation with programmable Fire Man Floor facility.
